Temporary profession of vows in the Order of Friars Minor is a sacred promise to live the Gospel in the spirit of St. Francis through poverty, chastity, and obedience for one year, renewable each year. It is a time of grace where the brother deepens his life of prayer, fraternity, and service as he continues to discern God’s call to a lifelong commitment.

About

Welcome to the official photography portfolio of the Province of San Antonio de Padua – Philippines. Here, we capture not just images, but stories—moments of grace, brotherhood, and the sacred rhythm of Franciscan life.

Rooted in the charism of St. Francis of Assisi, our mission is to visually document the journey of faith lived out in simplicity, fraternity, and joyful service.

Each frame reveals the quiet beauty of daily life as a friar, the tenderness of prayer, and the vibrant encounters with the people we serve. Through these images, we invite you to witness the profound mystery of God made visible in ordinary moments—moments where heaven touches earth.

"Preach the Gospel at all times; use visuals when necessary." — (a modern Franciscan twist)
